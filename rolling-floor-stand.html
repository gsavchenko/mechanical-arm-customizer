Features


The Rolling Floor Stand is an easy-to-use host for your Lift and Lock system. Simply move your Vertical Bar from your current set-up on to the Rolling Floor Stand. From there, your system will be able to follow you bedside, around a table or from room to room. The standard Rolling Floor Stand has five single legs that pivot to provide more manoeuvrability. The Leg Extensions model offers three single legs and two extended legs for added balance.

The Rolling Floor Stand allows your specific device that was mounted to a wheelchair to be used in a more flexible way. Now it can be accessible even when not attached to a wheelchair or power chair.
