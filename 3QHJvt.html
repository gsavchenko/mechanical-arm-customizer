F.A.Q.

Q: How do you explain the cost difference between your products and those bought from generic online sources?

A: Quality is something that we are unwilling to compromise on. We do our best to ensure that our prices are competitive with what else is out there but by using higher quality materials and not cutting corners the cost of manufacturing is going to have more influence in the final cost. We would much rather offer a durable, high quality product that will stand up in real life situations than offer something cheap that will fail after only hours of use. To help us do this, we are always open to feedback from our users that will help us improve our products and make using them the best experience possible.

Q: Once we’ve bought something from you, what kind of support can we expect?

A: We believe that our customer support is another feature that sets us apart from other companies. We understand that devices are only useful if you know how to use them. This is why we are here to walk you through any difficulties you might have with our systems. Simply give us a call or send us an email and we’ll make sure that you’re set up right.

Q: I’m interested in what you have to offer, but how do I know which of your products to use in my situation?

A: We want to make sure you have exactly what you need and that it is set up for maximum functionality. We offer a virtual system builder online to let you visualize what a potential mounting system might look like for you. If you are interested in products other than mounting systems, or you simply wish to speak to someone about your situation feel free to give us a call or send us an email.

Q: I have a device that needs to be mounted but it’s somewhat obscure, can you still help me out?

A: We take on custom design jobs all the time. If you have a device that we have not come across before we are always willing to take a look at it and see if we can figure something reasonable out.

Q: Do you offer services like consulting to assist with your devices?

A: Yes, we do. We always want to make sure our users understand our products fully. As a way of helping our clients make the right decisions when mounting devices and ensure that they are able to use these devices to their fullest potential we have people who are on the road, Monday to Friday. Some clients choose to have regularly scheduled meetings so that we might have someone on hand to meet with their patients while others might only need to meet with us once. Give us a call and we can talk about how to best meet your needs.

Q: Would you be willing to schedule a visit to our home/hospital/centre?

A: We regularly have representatives out on the road visiting clients. If you feel like a visit from us would be beneficial, give us a call to set up a time that works. We’d be happy to see you and to make sure your systems are set up right.

Q: Does Ideas sell its products outside of Canada?

A: Yes, we do sell outside of Canada. We have established various partnerships with companies in the United States that help us to deliver our products to clients south of the border. If you’re located in America and wish to place an order, contact us directly and we’ll make sure all the arrangements are in place.
